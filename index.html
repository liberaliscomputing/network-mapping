Welcome to MozFest Network Mapping!

Visualisation: Explore the MozFest network in real time

Four types of visualisations guide you to explore how the network looks like.

  • Concentric: Places people with more connections in levels toward the centre
  • CoSE-Bilkent: Displays the network in a classic way a.k.a. force layout
  • Circle: Randomly distributes people on a globe
  • Grid: Places people with more connections toward upper rows in a grid

Navigate and interact with the network.

  • Search people with Twitter handles (e.g. mozillafestival).
  • Hover over, click, or double-click nodes.

Connecting People: Meet with other participants

Take a look at participant cards to find whom you should connect with.

  • Chance Encounter: Randomly displays registered participants
  • Recent Registration: Displays recently registered participants
  • Community Bridge: Leaders bridging unconnected people (betweenness)
